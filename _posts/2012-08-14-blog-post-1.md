This is a deep learning project on 3D furniture design that I completed in my second year of undergraduate studies in collaboration with Guowei Xu.

Abstract
======
The costly and time-consuming nature of room design, combined with the ineffi-
ciencies of manual adjustments, inspired the development of DreamHouseAI to
automatically generate and refine designs based on user feedback. DreamHouseAI ,
powered by GPT-4o, which operates in two main stages: Instance Design and Tex-
ture Design. Initially, it generates furniture layouts based on user-provided floor
plans and requirements, then creates furniture appearances according to user stylis-
tic preferences, with real-time feedback optimization. Additionally, users can
transfer real-world furniture instances or specific home decor styles into the inter-
active environment, ensuring more practical and harmonious designs. The entire
process is meticulously designed to align with user needs, enabling automatic
generation and adjustment within different components based on multimodal in-
puts. DreamHouseAI bridges the gap between reality, user requirements, and ideal
design concepts, facilitating improved communication between users and designers,
and even potentially becoming our Personal Home Design Expert.
